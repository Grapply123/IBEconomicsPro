About the Economics IA

The Internal Assessment (IA) is a critical component of your IB Economics grade, accounting for 20% of your overall mark. It requires you to write three separate commentaries focusing on microeconomics, macroeconomics, and either international trade or development economics. EconIAPro is here to ensure you excel in every aspect of your IA!
