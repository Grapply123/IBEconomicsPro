Who I Am

Welcome to EconIAPro, a platform I created to help IB Economics students master their Internal Assessments and reach their highest potential. My name is Luigi Sciascia, and I’m an HL Economics student with two years of dedicated study in the subject. My journey in economics began not just with a curiosity about theory, but also with a desire to share knowledge and help my peers succeed.

I started tutoring fellow classmates early in my IB journey, focusing on breaking down complex economic concepts into simple ideas and providing specific feedback on their IAs. It was one of the most satisfying moments of my academic career to see their grades progress from 4s to 6s and even 7s. It made me realize just how much I enjoy teaching and the impact personalized guidance can have on others' success.
