Choosing the Right Article

Selecting the right article is a critical first step in mastering your IA. Here are some tips to help you choose a suitable article:

  • Make sure the article comes from a reputable news source (e.g., BBC, CNN, The Guardian).
  • Choose an article published within the last 12 months to meet IB guidelines.
  • Look for articles discussing the imposition of a policy (e.g., taxes, subsidies) rather than simply describing a situation.

By selecting an article that aligns with these criteria, you'll set a strong foundation for your IA.
